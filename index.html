Common Eligibility Criteria:

Family Income:
Your total family income must be below a certain limit, often falling within the Economically Weaker Section (EWS) guidelines.
Academic Performance:
A minimum academic score, such as 6.5 CPI for Degree students or 60% marks for Diploma students, in the previous academic year.
No Other Scholarships:
You cannot apply if you already receive another scholarship covering full fees, hostel, and mess charges.
Commonly Required Documents:
Income Certificate, SSC and HSC Mark Sheets, Yearly Results, Domicile Certificate, Aadhar Card, DTE Letter, Fee Receipt.
