Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

⬅ Back to Scholarship Form
1. How do I apply for a scholarship?

Visit the official website or the National Scholarship Portal (scholarships.gov.in), log in, and fill out the required application form with the necessary documents.

2. What documents are needed for the scholarship?

You’ll typically need income certificates, mark sheets, Aadhaar card, fee receipts, and category certificates. See the Documents Page for details.

3. How can I track my scholarship application status?

You can track it through the official portal where you applied. Status updates are usually available in your dashboard after login.

4. What if my scholarship is delayed?

If your payment or approval is delayed, you can fill out the Grievance Form to report the issue.

5. Who should I contact for technical issues?

You can contact your institute’s scholarship cell or the support email mentioned on the scholarship website.

6. Can I apply for more than one scholarship?

No, generally you can apply for only one scholarship that covers your fees and expenses fully.
