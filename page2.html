OPEN / General (Common)

  • Income certificate (if required by scheme)
  • Aadhar Card (or other ID proof)
  • SSC & HSC / previous year mark sheets
  • College fee receipts / DTE allotment letter
  • Bank passbook copy (for direct transfer)

SC / ST

  • Caste certificate (SC/ST) issued by competent authority
  • Income certificate (if scholarship requires income limit)
  • Academic mark sheets
  • Aadhar card and passport-size photograph

OBC / SBC / VJNT

  • Category certificate (OBC/SBC/VJNT)
  • Income certificate (for schemes with income cap)
  • Academic mark sheets and fee receipts
  • Aadhar card & bank details

EWS (Economically Weaker Section)

  • EWS certificate / Income & asset declaration
  • Academic mark sheets
  • Aadhar / ID proof
  • College fee receipt

Minority

  • Minority community certificate
  • Income certificate
  • Mark sheets and ID proof
  • Any other document specified by the scheme
